About Us

We specialize in mutton & lambshank biryani!

We serve delicious Dum biryani that are originally known at Ayer Rajah Food Centre where we are awarded the 'Top Heritage Award 2021', appeared in the TV show 'Biryani Khakis'. We then have shifted from Ayer Rajah Food Centre in 2022. We are now located at Pasir Panjang Food Centre #01-44 & Jalan Berseh Food Centre #02-54. 'Dum' refers to the slow cooking of rice with meat to absorb the umami flavor and enrich the rice.
